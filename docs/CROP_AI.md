# Crop AI

**Version:** `4.3.3-enterprise`  
**Sprint:** 14.3  
**Foundation:** Enterprise Platform v4.3.2-enterprise  
**API:** `/api/crop-ai/v1`  
**Package:** `applications/agro_enterprise/crop_ai/`

Enterprise AI for crop intelligence, disease detection, autonomous farm operations, and predictive yield management.

## Domains

| Domain | Capabilities |
|--------|----------------|
| Crop Intelligence | Registry, growth stages, health, phenology, performance, harvest readiness |
| Disease Detection AI | Leaf/stem/root/fruit, fungal/bacterial/viral, severity, treatment |
| Pest Intelligence | Identification, infestation prediction, risk mapping, biological control |
| Yield Intelligence | Yield/quality/loss prediction, harvest forecast, regional & historical analytics |
| Autonomous Ops | Task planning, mission scheduler, drone/machinery assignment, spray/fertilize/harvest |
| AI Decision Support | Crop/treatment/nutrient/harvest/risk/operations recommendations |
| Dashboards | Crop health, disease, pest, yield, farm ops, AI recommendations |
| Knowledge | Crop/disease/pest/yield/autonomous ops registries |

Does **not** modify Platform Core, AI OS, Enterprise Edition, Automotive, or previous Agro sprint packages.
