# Multimodal Transport

**Version:** `4.5.3-enterprise`  
**Sprint:** 15.3  
**Foundation:** Enterprise Platform v4.5.2-enterprise  
**Package:** `applications/port_enterprise/multimodal_logistics/`  
**API:** `/api/port-multimodal/v1`

## Capabilities

- Transport chain management across sea, rail, truck, barge, air
- Mode transfer planning and container transfer
- Intermodal terminals, cross-docking, consolidation
- Transport optimization and inland dry ports / DCs / hubs

## Endpoints

| Method | Path | Purpose |
|--------|------|---------|
| GET/POST | `/multimodal` | Chains, transfers, intermodal, cross-dock, optimize |
| GET/POST | `/inland` | Dry ports, DCs, hubs, redistribution, storage |
