## Principles

- AI analyzes, forecasts, and recommends only
- No automatic commercial actions (customers, finance, pricing, staff, marketing)
- Owner makes the final decision
- Universal across Beauty, Cafe, Construction, Retail, Manufacturing, Crypto, and more
