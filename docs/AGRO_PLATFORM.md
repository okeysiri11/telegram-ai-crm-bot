# Agro Enterprise Platform

**Version:** `4.3.1-enterprise`  
**Sprint:** 14.0 (+ 14.1 Precision Agriculture)  
**Foundation:** Enterprise Platform v4.3.0-enterprise  
**API:** `/api/agro-enterprise/v1`  
**Package:** `applications/agro_enterprise/`

Foundation of the Agro Enterprise Platform — AI-powered agricultural marketplace, farm registry, crop management, CRM, knowledge, and executive dashboards.

## Domains

| Domain | Capabilities |
|--------|----------------|
| Agro Marketplace | Crops/seeds/fertilizers/equipment/services buy & sell, supplier & buyer directories |
| Farm Registry | Farms, companies, farmland, storage, equipment, livestock, certifications |
| Crop Management | Catalog, seasons, rotation, field assignment, yield & harvest plans, calendar |
| Enterprise CRM | Farmer/supplier/buyer CRM, contracts, leads, tasks, calendar |
| Knowledge | Crop, soil, equipment, regulations + knowledge graph nodes |
| Executive Dashboards | Marketplace, farm, production, sales, executive KPIs |

Does **not** modify Platform Core, AI OS, Enterprise Edition, Automotive Enterprise Platform, or legacy `agro_marketplace`.

See: [AGRO_MARKETPLACE.md](AGRO_MARKETPLACE.md), [FARM_REGISTRY.md](FARM_REGISTRY.md), [CROP_MANAGEMENT.md](CROP_MANAGEMENT.md).
