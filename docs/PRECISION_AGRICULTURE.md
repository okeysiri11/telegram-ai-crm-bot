# Precision Agriculture

**Version:** `4.3.2-enterprise`  
**Sprint:** 14.1  
**Foundation:** Enterprise Platform v4.3.1-enterprise  
**API:** `/api/precision-agriculture/v1`  
**Package:** `applications/agro_enterprise/precision_agriculture/`

Enterprise Precision Agriculture — GIS, drones, satellite imagery, IoT sensors, AI field monitoring, and smart farming.

## Domains

| Domain | Capabilities |
|--------|----------------|
| Field Management | Registry, boundaries, GPS, history, ownership, crop/soil, analytics |
| GIS | Interactive maps, satellite/topo/NDVI/moisture/elevation/historical layers |
| Drone | Mission planning, survey, orthomosaic, multispectral/thermal, plant counting, Drone Platform bridge |
| Satellite | Imagery, NDVI/NDRE, vegetation & moisture, weather overlay, timeline |
| IoT Smart Farming | Weather/soil/temp/humidity/water sensors, irrigation, sensor dashboard |
| AI Crop Monitoring | Disease/pest/nutrient/weed, growth stage, yield & harvest readiness |
| Dashboards | Field, drone, satellite, IoT, crop health |
| Knowledge | Field/crop/drone/satellite/sensor registries |

Does **not** modify Platform Core, AI OS, Enterprise Edition, Automotive, or Drone Platform packages.
