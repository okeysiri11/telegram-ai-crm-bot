# Smart Irrigation

**Version:** `4.3.3-enterprise`  
**Sprint:** 14.2  
**Foundation:** Enterprise Platform v4.3.2-enterprise  
**API:** `/api/smart-irrigation/v1`  
**Package:** `applications/agro_enterprise/smart_irrigation/`

Enterprise irrigation, soil intelligence, and water management with AI optimization and IoT.

## Domains

| Domain | Capabilities |
|--------|----------------|
| Soil Intelligence | Registry, composition, fertility, nutrients, OM, pH, salinity, compaction, history |
| Water Management | Sources, reservoirs/canals, consumption, balance, quality, groundwater |
| Smart Irrigation | Zones, scheduler, valves/pumps, flow/pressure, leak detection, remote control |
| IoT Sensors | Moisture/temp/humidity/rain/solar/wind, gateways, health monitoring |
| AI Irrigation | Demand prediction, recommendations, ET, drought risk, cost optimization |
| Environmental AI | Weather, climate/microclimate, water/heat stress, flood risk |
| Dashboards | Irrigation, water, soil, sensor, AI recommendation |
| Knowledge | Soil/water/irrigation/sensor/environmental registries |

Does **not** modify Platform Core, AI OS, Enterprise Edition, Automotive, or previous Agro sprint packages.
