# Enterprise Product Intelligence

**Version:** `6.1.0`  
**Sprint:** 22.0  
**API:** `/api/enterprise-epi/v1`  
**Library:** `platform_product_intelligence/`  
**Hub attr:** `enterprise_hub.product_intelligence`  
**Design path:** `src/modules/product-intelligence` → `platform_product_intelligence/`

Mandatory decision center for platform evolution. All modules can submit feedback, bugs, and ideas. AI analyzes and advises; the platform owner alone approves changes. AI never modifies the system autonomously.

## Principles

- AI never independently changes the system
- Expert analysis before decisions
- Owner makes the final call
- Every feature has measurable expected effect
- Post-release validation required
- Full decision history retained
