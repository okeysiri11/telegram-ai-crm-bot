# Legal Search

**Version:** `4.9.1-enterprise`  
**API:** `POST /api/legal-li/v1/search`

## Modes

- Semantic Search
- Natural Language Search
- Article Search
- Keyword Search
- Cross-Reference Search
- Citation Search
- Related Law Discovery

## Example

```json
{"action": "semantic", "query": "data protection controller", "limit": 5}
```
