# Navigation Management

**Version:** `4.5.1-enterprise`  
**Sprint:** 15.1  
**Package:** `applications/port_enterprise/navigation/`

## Capabilities

- Navigation routes and fairway management
- Pilot boarding zones and anchorage areas
- Navigation restrictions, weather overlay, sea state monitoring
- AI traffic prediction, optimal routing, arrival optimization, berth recommendation
