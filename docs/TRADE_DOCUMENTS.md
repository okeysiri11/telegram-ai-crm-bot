# Trade Documents

**Version:** `4.5.4-enterprise`  
**Sprint:** 15.4  
**Foundation:** Enterprise Platform v4.5.3-enterprise  
**Package:** `applications/port_enterprise/customs_trade/`  
**API:** `/api/port-customs/v1`

## Capabilities

- Electronic document repository
- Bill of Lading, CMR, rail/air waybills
- Certificate of origin, phytosanitary & veterinary certificates
- Electronic signatures
- Executive dashboards: customs, border, trade, compliance, AI trade

## Endpoints

| Method | Path | Purpose |
|--------|------|---------|
| GET/POST | `/documents` | Store / sign trade documents |
| GET/POST | `/dashboard` | Customs / border / trade / compliance / AI dashboards |
| GET/POST | `/knowledge` | Trade knowledge graph registries |

Document Management Ready (within Customs & Trade platform)
