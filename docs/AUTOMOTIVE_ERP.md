# Automotive ERP

**Version:** `4.1.6-enterprise`  
**Sprint:** 13.6  
**Foundation:** Enterprise Platform v4.1.5-enterprise  
**API:** `/api/automotive-erp/v1`

Complete Automotive ERP for dealerships, service centers, fleet operators, logistics companies, and enterprise customers.

## Domains

| Domain | Capabilities |
|--------|----------------|
| Service Center | Service/repair orders, scheduler, mechanics, workshop calendar, history, warranty, QC |
| Fleet | Dashboard, assignment, drivers, trips, fuel, maintenance schedule, health, utilization |
| Parts | Catalog, warehouse, stock, suppliers, POs, reservations, serial tracking, forecast |
| Maintenance AI | Predictive maintenance, failure/downtime prediction, repair recommendations, cost forecast |
| Enterprise ERP | CRM/accounting hooks, procurement, invoices, contracts, customer & employee portals |
| Analytics | Fleet, service, cost, profit, inventory, executive KPIs |
| Integrations | VIN Intelligence, Inspection AI, Dealer CRM, Buyer/Seller AI, Marketplace, Executive Dashboard, AI OS |

Does **not** modify Platform Core, AI OS, Enterprise Edition, or previous sprint packages.
