# Business Intelligence

**Version:** `5.1.5-enterprise`  
**API:** `GET/POST /api/finance-rpt/v1/intelligence`

## Capabilities

- Financial KPI Engine
- Revenue / Expense Analytics
- Margin & Profitability Analysis
- Trend Analysis
- Variance Analytics

Register KPIs with `action: kpi`; run analytics with `analytic_type` (revenue, expense, margin, profitability, trend, variance).
