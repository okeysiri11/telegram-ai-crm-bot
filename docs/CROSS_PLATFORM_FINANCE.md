# Cross-Platform Finance

**Version:** `5.1.7-enterprise`  
**API:** `GET/POST /api/finance-int/v1/platforms`

## Adapters

| Platform | Sample operations |
|----------|-------------------|
| automotive | vehicle sales, payments, service invoices, parts valuation, warranty, dealer settlement |
| agro | crop sales, warehouse valuation, supplier payments, harvest cost, export settlement, subsidies |
| port | shipping invoices, cargo cost, terminal fees, logistics, export docs, port settlement |
| crypto | digital asset settlement, OTC, treasury/wallet sync, exchange settlement, stablecoin |
| legal | contract billing, legal fees, court costs, compliance, licenses, expense allocation |
