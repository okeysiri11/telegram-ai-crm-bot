# Enterprise Reporting

**Version:** `5.1.5-enterprise`  
**API:** `/api/finance-rpt/v1/consolidation` · `/api/finance-rpt/v1/forecast`

## Consolidation

Multi-Company Consolidation · Intercompany Elimination · Consolidated Financial Statements · Group Performance Analysis · Cross-Platform Financial Analytics

## Forecasting & Scenarios

Revenue · Profit · Cash Flow · Liquidity forecasts · Scenario simulation · Sensitivity analysis

Does not modify Automotive, Agro, Port, Crypto, or Legal platforms — cross-platform analytics are declarative rollups only.
